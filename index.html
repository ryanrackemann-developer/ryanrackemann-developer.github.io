Why Choose Us

Looking for an agency that works with globally recognized brands and boasts their work with fortune 500 companies?

If you’re looking for that agency, that isn’t us.

When we founded 3 Designed Marketing in 2015, we saw how largely underserved small businesses were in the marketplace. We see the ridiculous retainers that agencies charge their clients. While their agency structures and pricing models worked well for large corporations, often it does not adequately serve small to medium sized businesses. We started this agency with one goal in mind – to help small businesses grow. And since then, we’ve continued to produce results for our clients.
